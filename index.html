loyola high school

Loyola High School and Junior College is a private Catholic primary and secondary school located in Pashan, Pune, India. Founded by the Jesuits in 1961, the school provides a single-sex education for boys only from K to Grade X; and a co-educational environment in the pre-university college, for grades XI and XII.

The Platinum Jubilee celebrations of Loyola High

School, Margao commenced on 23rd February 2019, with a solemn Eucharistic thanksgiving mass at Our Lady of Grace Church, Margao. The Principal of Loyola High School, Rev. Fr. Basil Vago SJ, led the celebrations along with the management, staff, students, ex-principals, ex-staff,, ex-students, the Loyola Ex Students Association (LESA) and well-wishers on the theme ‘Called to build and serve humanity through education’.



To mark the occasion, a Jubilee Plaque was unveiled by the Chief Guest Rev. Fr. William Rodrigues, (the Socius to the Provincial of the Goa Jesuits). A special Jubilee Anthem was composed and sung by ex-students of the school. Along with celebrations, magnificent display of colour and costumes enthralled the august gathering as students presented a cultural programme packed with song and dance at the school premises on Saturday evening. To commemorate the 75 years of the institution, a total of 75 balloons were also released while festive tunes from a brass band stepped up the tempo on the grand celebrations.



In an homily speech by the former Principal of Loyola High School, Rev. Fr. William Rodrigues SJ, the journey of Loyola High School since 1944 to the present day was recalled as an institution of excellence, moulding young boys to become great men for others. He hailed the achievements of the school in the all round development of the students in the foundation, their character formation, religious tolerance, and moral integrity etc.



Rev. Fr. Basil Vago and Rev Fr Simon de Melo (Vice Principal) thanked the PTA, Jubilee Organizing Committee, LESA, staff and students for their wholehearted support and co-operation in planning the inaugural function of the Platinum Jubilee celebrations and upcoming events. To mark this occasion the school has marked a plethora of events for this year with like competitions and event for Inter-school and Higher Secondary, a marathon, workshop for teachers by very eminent resource persons, outreach programmes and staff capacity building programmes through exchange programmes.
